A consultant specialising in safety & reliability analysis servicing a wide variety of industries. I assist clients with deterministic & probabilistic analysis, or in layman's terms measuring risk based on known physical limits, environmental conditions, or historical events.

In either situation, the knowledge acquired from past projects allows me to assist my clients in the best possible way that experience affords.
